A read-only console over the vault. Five artefacts. One asks where the graph is dense. The other four turn the schema into questions: how the law converges, when the events landed, whether the forecasts have shipped, and what two sources say side by side.

Force-directed graph of every wikilink in the vault. Hover for a one-line summary; click for the side panel.

Convergence

Jurisdiction × doctrine matrix. Three jurisdictions, eight doctrines; the cells are the cases where they agree, disagree, or are silent.

Events on a horizontal time axis, in lanes (regulation, pilot, academic, incident). The shape of the field, year by year.

Realised vs Projected

Bull-case sizing forecasts beside realised AUM. Honest accounting of what shipped, what didn't, and what the sceptics say.

→ 05

Compare

Pick any two notes. The frontmatter aligns on the left, the body panels align on the right. For MiCA vs GENIUS, UCC Art 12 vs UK Property Act, BUIDL vs BENJI.
