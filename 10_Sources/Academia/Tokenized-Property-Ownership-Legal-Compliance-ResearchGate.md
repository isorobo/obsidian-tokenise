## Annotation

This document serves as a **scholarly publication** focused on the intersection of **digital assets and real estate law**. The primary goal of the text is to analyse the **regulatory requirements** and formal **legal acknowledgment** necessary to validate the division of physical assets into digital units. By exploring a **compliance framework**, the authors aim to establish a structured approach for managing **blockchain-based real estate transactions** within existing legal systems. Consequently, the research provides a foundation for understanding how **emerging technologies** can safely integrate with traditional property rights.
