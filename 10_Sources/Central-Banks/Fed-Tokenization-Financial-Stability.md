## Annotation

This Federal Reserve discussion paper provides a comprehensive overview of **tokenisation**, the process of creating **digital representations of real-world assets** on a blockchain. The authors explore how linking physical or financial assets, such as gold, real estate, and government bonds, to crypto tokens can lower investment barriers and **enhance market liquidity**. However, the text warns that these innovations create deep **interconnections between digital and traditional finance**, potentially allowing volatility to bridge the two ecosystems. The study concludes that while the current market remains small, future growth could threaten **financial stability** through redemption risks, potential bank runs, and the rapid transmission of market shocks.
