# HSBC Orion Blockchain Platform

**Source:** [https://www.about.hsbc.com.hk/](https://www.about.hsbc.com.hk/)

**Register entry:** [[SOURCE-REGISTER]]

## Annotation

HSBC Orion is a specialised **institutional distributed-ledger platform** designed to manage the entire lifecycle of **digitally native bonds**, from their initial issuance to final redemption. Rather than operating as a public cryptocurrency network, it serves as a **regulated environment** for financial entities to trade and settle tokenised debt with increased operational efficiency. The platform is notable for its **legal and technical alignment** with Luxembourg two-tier account structure and its significant role in the **UK Digital Gilt Instrument pilot**. By successfully facilitating over **USD 3.5 billion in global bond issuances**, Orion demonstrates the practical shift toward integrating **tokenised assets and cash legs** for faster, more secure institutional financial markets.
