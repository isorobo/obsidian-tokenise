## Annotation

Through a collaborative initiative known as Project Guardian, financial leaders explored how **decentralised finance** can be adapted for regulated, institutional use cases. By testing a modified version of the Aave protocol, researchers successfully demonstrated that **tokenised assets** could be traded and settled on public blockchains while maintaining strict compliance through **verifiable credentials**. These experimental findings directly informed the creation of **Aave Horizon**, a hybrid marketplace designed to bridge traditional capital with digital liquidity. Ultimately, the text illustrates the evolution of **institutional DeFi**, showcasing how open-source technology can be refined to support professional-grade security and real-world asset integration.
