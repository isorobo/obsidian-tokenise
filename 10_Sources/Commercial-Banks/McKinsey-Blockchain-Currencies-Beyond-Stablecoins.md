## Annotation

This McKinsey report details a shift in digital finance where **tokenised bank deposits** are surpassing stablecoins in institutional utility and transaction volume. While stablecoins are often viewed as the face of digital currency, the text argues that a more significant **three-layer monetary stack** is emerging, consisting of stablecoins for retail agility, tokenised deposits for commercial banking, and **central bank digital currencies** for final risk-free settlement. The authors highlight that bank-led tokenisation preserves traditional balance sheets and **regulatory compliance** while offering the speed and programmability of blockchain. Ultimately, the purpose of the article is to outline the **interoperability challenges** and strategic frameworks necessary to integrate these diverse digital assets into a unified global financial system.
