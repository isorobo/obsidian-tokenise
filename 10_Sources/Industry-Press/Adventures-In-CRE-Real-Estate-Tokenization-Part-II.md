## Annotation

This resource serves as a practical blueprint for **real estate tokenisation**, detailing the transition from a theoretical concept to a functional **capital stack** strategy. The text outlines a multi-step execution process that involves establishing a **Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)** to hold property titles, selecting a **regulatory jurisdiction**, and deploying **smart contracts** to automate distributions. By leveraging **blockchain infrastructure**, sponsors can fractionalise high-value assets to enhance **secondary market liquidity** and streamline administrative operations through digital **KYC/AML** onboarding. While highlighting major global platforms and evolving legal frameworks like **MiCA** and **VARA**, the guide ultimately emphasises that tokenisation is a sophisticated **infrastructure upgrade** rather than a way to bypass traditional real estate risks or securities laws.
