# UK Selects HSBC Blockchain Platform for Digital Bond Pilot (CoinGeek)

**Source:** [https://coingeek.com/](https://coingeek.com/)

## Annotation

The United Kingdom has appointed HSBC to facilitate a landmark pilot program known as **DIGIT**, which explores the issuance of **sovereign debt using blockchain technology**. By utilising the **HSBC Orion platform**, the government aims to modernise its financial infrastructure and test the efficiency of **digitally native bonds** within a controlled regulatory environment. This initiative is designed to **streamline settlement processes** and reduce transaction costs, potentially positioning the UK as a global leader in the **tokenisation of government securities**. Ultimately, the project serves as a strategic effort to foster **financial innovation** and attract international investment through more transparent and agile debt management systems.
