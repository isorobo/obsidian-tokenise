## Annotation

This text provides a comprehensive overview of **Agent-Blockchain Interoperability**, a field dedicated to integrating **autonomous software agents** with **distributed ledger technology** to ensure secure and coordinated actions. The source outlines a structural framework consisting of **layered architectural models**, decentralised identity registries, and **smart-contract-driven workflows** that manage everything from discovery to financial settlement. By utilising **cryptographic authentication** and immutable audit trails, these systems establish a **trustless environment** where heterogeneous agents can engage in complex economic marketplaces and robotic coordination. Ultimately, the document serves as a roadmap for developing a **decentralised agentic web**, highlighting how blockchain solves critical challenges in security, governance, and **cross-domain communication** for artificial intelligence.
