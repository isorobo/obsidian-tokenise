## Annotation

The provided text examines how **stablecoins** serve as the essential financial infrastructure for converting physical property and financial instruments into **digital tokens**. By offering a consistent value pegged to fiat currency, these digital assets facilitate **instant on-chain settlements** and provide the necessary liquidity to make once-illiquid markets, like real estate or gold, easily tradable. The author highlights that the integration of **programmable finance** allows for automated dividends and reduced counterparty risk, effectively bridging the gap between traditional banking and decentralised ecosystems. Ultimately, the article positions stablecoins as the **foundational layer** that transforms global commerce into a more transparent, accessible, and 24/7 digital economy.
