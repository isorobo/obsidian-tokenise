## Annotation

The banking giant HSBC has successfully tested the transfer of **tokenised deposits** on the public Canton Network, signalling a major step in the **digitalisation of traditional assets**. This simulated trial achieved **atomic settlement**, allowing for the instantaneous and secure exchange of cash for other digital securities while maintaining the **configurable privacy** required by global financial institutions. By expanding these services into the United States market, the bank is positioning itself at the forefront of **blockchain-based infrastructure** for institutional finance. Ultimately, the text highlights how major lenders are navigating **regulatory frameworks** to balance the efficiency of public distributed ledgers with the strict oversight of the banking sector.
