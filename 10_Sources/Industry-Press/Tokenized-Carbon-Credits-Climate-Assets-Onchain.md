## Annotation

This source provides a comprehensive overview of how **tokenised carbon credits** serve as a technological bridge between traditional environmental offsets and the digital economy. The text outlines a structural transition from fragmented legacy registries to **transparent onchain ledgers**, highlighting how smart contracts can automate the retirement of credits to prevent issues like double counting. Central to this ecosystem is the use of **Chainlink oracles and decentralised data**, which facilitate accurate measurement and verification of real-world ecological impacts. Ultimately, the document serves to educate institutional and developer audiences on how **blockchain interoperability** and programmable assets can create a more liquid, efficient, and accountable global climate market.
