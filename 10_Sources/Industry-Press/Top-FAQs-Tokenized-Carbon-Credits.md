## Annotation

This resource provides a comprehensive guide to **tokenised carbon credits**, explaining how blockchain technology can transform environmental offsets into **programmable digital assets** to enhance market liquidity and transparency. The text emphasises that these tokens do not replace **traditional registries** like Verra or Gold Standard; instead, they serve as a digital bridge that requires strict synchronisation to prevent **double-counting** and ensure the integrity of the retirement process. By addressing technical challenges and **regulatory considerations**, the guide outlines how organisations like Zoniqx facilitate **compliant tokenisation frameworks** while managing risks such as market misalignment and smart-contract security. Ultimately, the document serves as both an educational FAQ and a strategic overview of the **lifecycle and evaluation** of blockchain-based environmental commodities for modern corporate sustainability.
