## Annotation

This article explores the evolution of digital currency into **RWA stablecoins**, a sophisticated asset class backed by **tangible off-chain holdings** such as gold and government treasuries. By contrasting these with traditional fiat-backed tokens, the text highlights how this new model offers **on-chain access to yield** and institutional-grade collateral within a **programmable financial ecosystem**. The author details the current market landscape by identifying **major institutional players** and the regulatory frameworks accelerating adoption. Ultimately, the source serves as a strategic overview of how **tokenised real-world assets** are bridging the gap between traditional finance and decentralised technology to create more productive, liquid capital.
