## Annotation

The source explores how the financial industry is evolving through the **tokenisation of Real World Assets (RWA)**, which converts physical holdings into digital blockchain tokens to address the high costs and limited accessibility of traditional systems. By enabling **fractional ownership** and reducing the need for centralised intermediaries, this shift allows a broader range of investors to participate in markets like real estate, bonds, and private credit. The text identifies **stablecoins** as a critical foundation for this ecosystem, providing a non-volatile medium for global value transfer and automated settlements. Finally, it highlights the strategic role of **Wallet-as-a-Service (WaaS)** as the necessary infrastructure that simplifies technical complexities and ensures regulatory compliance, ultimately paving the way for the **mass adoption of digital finance**.
