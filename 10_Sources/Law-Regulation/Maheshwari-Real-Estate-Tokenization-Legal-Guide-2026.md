## Annotation

This article serves as a comprehensive legal guide for the 2026 landscape of **real estate tokenisation**, detailing how property rights are converted into **digital assets**. It explains that property is not held directly on a blockchain but through **Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs)** that treat tokens as **regulated securities** to ensure investor protection. The text highlights critical **global regulatory frameworks**, such as India SM REITs, EU MiCA, and the US Uniform Commercial Code, which together provide a standardised roadmap for **fractional ownership**. Furthermore, it introduces the **ERC-3643 standard** as a vital tool for embedding legal compliance and identity verification directly into smart contracts. Ultimately, the source argues that while technology increases **market liquidity and transparency**, the industry must remain anchored in established **property law and statutory compliance**.
