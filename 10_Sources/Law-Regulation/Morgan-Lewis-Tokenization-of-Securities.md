## Annotation

This presentation by Morgan Lewis explores the **tokenisation of securities**, a transformative process that converts traditional financial instruments into **digital representations** on a blockchain. By utilising **smart contracts**, self-executing code that automates governance and transfers, this technology offers significant advantages such as **around-the-clock trading**, enhanced transparency, and **accelerated settlement times**. A core objective of this shift is to **streamline record-keeping** by collapsing complex, multi-layered ownership ledgers into a single, unified blockchain record. Ultimately, the text serves as a high-level guide for navigating the **regulatory and commercial law** complexities that arise when integrating traditional fund shares into the modern **digital asset ecosystem**.
