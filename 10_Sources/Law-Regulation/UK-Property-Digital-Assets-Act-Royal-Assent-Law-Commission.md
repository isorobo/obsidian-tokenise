## Annotation

The Property (Digital Assets etc) Act 2025 marks a transformative shift in legal history by formally recognising **digital assets as personal property** under the law of England and Wales. Because items like cryptocurrency and NFTs do not fit into traditional definitions of physical goods or legal debts, this legislation establishes a distinct **third category of property** to accommodate their unique virtual nature. By removing previous ambiguities, the Act provides **enhanced legal certainty** and protection for owners, ensuring that the court system can treat these emerging technologies with the same rigor as conventional assets. Ultimately, this reform serves to modernise the legal framework, positioning the jurisdiction as a **global leader in digital commerce** and innovation.
