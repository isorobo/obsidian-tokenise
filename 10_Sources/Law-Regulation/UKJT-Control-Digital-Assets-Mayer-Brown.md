## Annotation

The UK Jurisdiction Taskforce has released a pivotal report detailing how the legal concept of **factual control** applies to the management of **digital assets** under English law. Because virtual holdings often bypass traditional definitions of property, the text introduces a **third category of personal property** where control serves as the functional equivalent of physical possession. The document provides a practical framework for judges and lawyers to identify who holds authority over an asset, whether through **private keys, smart contracts, or third-party custodians**. By clarifying these technical mechanisms, the report aims to provide **greater legal certainty** for stakeholders navigating digital trading and complex jurisdictional disputes.
