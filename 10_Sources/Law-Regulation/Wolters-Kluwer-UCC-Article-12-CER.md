## Annotation

The provided source examines the 2022 amendments to the **Uniform Commercial Code**, specifically focusing on the introduction of **Article 12** to govern the burgeoning **digital asset** marketplace. The text explains the legal creation of **Controllable Electronic Records (CERs)**, a new category of intangible property that allows for the **perfection of security interests** through control rather than traditional filing. By establishing **uniform state-level rules** for the transfer and leveraging of assets like cryptocurrency and NFTs, the framework aims to reduce risk and provide **predictable commercial protections** for purchasers and lenders. Ultimately, the article serves as a strategic guide for **legal and financial professionals** to modernise their workflows and capitalise on the growing integration of **blockchain technology** within the global economy.
