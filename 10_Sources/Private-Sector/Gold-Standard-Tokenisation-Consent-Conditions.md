# Conditions for Consenting to Tokenisation of Gold Standard-issued Credits

**Source:** [https://www.goldstandard.org/](https://www.goldstandard.org/)

## Annotation

Gold Standard is establishing a **formal framework** to regulate the **tokenisation of carbon credits**, ensuring that digital representations of these assets on a blockchain are managed with high **environmental integrity**. While recognising that blockchain technology can improve **transparency and finance mobilisation**, the organisation has restricted unauthorised digital conversions to mitigate risks such as **regulatory uncertainty** and **reputational harm**. The document outlines a **collaborative consultation process** designed to gather stakeholder feedback on the specific criteria and conditions required for granting **express written consent** for these digital assets. Ultimately, this initiative seeks to balance technological innovation with a **controlled and responsible approach** to evolving carbon market instruments.
