# Legally Compliant Ownership of Tokenized Real Estate (RealT)

**Source:** [https://realt.co/](https://realt.co/)
**NotebookLM:** `b0b33ce3-331a-42da-ae53-cca3134d3cab` in [tokenise](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/d9fa07bb-4802-4626-8855-ba899655ab2b)
**Register entry:** [[SOURCE-REGISTER]]

## Annotation

RealT introduces a sophisticated framework for the **tokenisation of real estate**, utilising a Delaware **series limited liability company** to bridge the gap between physical property and the Ethereum blockchain. By dividing property ownership into **fractionalised ERC-20 tokens**, the system democratises access to real estate, significantly reducing the high costs and **illiquidity** typically associated with the industry. The document outlines a comprehensive ecosystem where third-party managers handle daily operations while investors retain **legal ownership rights**, including the ability to receive **daily rental payments** in decentralised stablecoins. Ultimately, this structure seeks to transform real estate into a highly **transactable digital asset** that remains fully compliant with **US securities laws** and regulatory requirements.
