# Verra Addresses Crypto Instruments and Tokens

## Annotation

The leading carbon-standard organisation, Verra, has issued a formal policy update regarding the intersection of **digital assets and environmental credits**. To maintain the **integrity of climate benefits**, the organisation has banned the conversion of retired carbon credits into crypto tokens, arguing that retirement signifies the final consumption of a credit impact. Instead, Verra proposes a more transparent path forward by **immobilising active credits** in its registry to allow for secure tokenisation. To refine this transition, the group is launching a **public consultation** to address security protocols, identity verification, and legal frameworks. Ultimately, this move aims to foster **innovation in carbon markets** while preventing fraud and double-counting in the emerging digital economy.
