## Annotation

This report explores the rising **institutional adoption of tokenised deposits**, positioning them as a regulated and more reliable alternative to stablecoins for modernising **interbank and cross-border payments**. Major global banks and regulatory bodies are shifting toward these **blockchain-native representations of commercial bank money** to enhance liquidity management and achieve near-instantaneous settlement speeds. The text highlights significant pilot programs from industry leaders like JPMorgan and HSBC, while emphasising that **regulatory clarity and interoperability** remain essential hurdles for scaling these digital assets. Ultimately, the source illustrates how the financial sector is integrating **distributed ledger technology** into traditional banking frameworks to create a more efficient and secure global value chain.
