# MOC - Private-Law Convergence

> **Cross-cutting MOC.** Three jurisdictions, three doctrines, one functional regime.

The USA, the UK, and the international private-law community have each landed on a workable doctrine for tokenised property between 2022 and 2025 - by structurally different routes. This MOC threads the convergence.

## The three legs

| Jurisdiction | Instrument | Doctrinal move |
|---|---|---|
| **USA** | 2022 UCC Amendments, **Article 12** | Creates *controllable electronic records*; adapts secured-transactions and take-free rules to digital assets. |
| **UK** | **Property (Digital Assets etc) Act 2025** | Statutorily confirms a **third category** of personal property beyond *things in possession* and *things in action*. Defers boundary-drawing to the courts. |
| **International** | **UNIDROIT Principles on Digital Assets and Private Law (2023)** | Nineteen jurisdiction-neutral principles covering control, transfer, custody, secured transactions, insolvency, choice of law. |
| **Civil-law tradition** | Academic | *Direct* (token *is* the right) vs *indirect* (token *represents* the right) tokenisation. Animates most cross-jurisdictional confusion. |
